Total Population Stats from 1960 - 2018

What do you think about the world's population from these graphs?

According to the UN World Data Bank, the world’s population is 7.6 billion and is expected to reach 8.6 billion in 2030, 9.8 billion by 2050 and 11.2 billion in 2100. Roughly, 83 million people are being added to the world’s population every year, the upward trend in population size is expected to continue, even assuming that fertility levels will continue to decline.
